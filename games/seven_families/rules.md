# 7 Familles

## Players
2 to N players (default 4)

## OBJECT OF THE GAME

Collect complete families by asking opponents for the cards you need. The player who completes the most families wins.

## HOW TO PLAY

The game is played with a deck of **n_families × n_components** cards divided into distinct families, each containing a fixed number of unique components. Cards are dealt evenly among all players; any remainder forms a face-down draw pile.

On your turn, choose any opponent and ask them for a **specific card** (you must name both the family and the component). You may only ask for a card from a family you already hold at least one card of.

- **Hit** — the opponent has that card: they give it to you and **you play again**.
- **Miss** — the opponent does not have that card: you draw the top card of the draw pile (if any), then your turn ends.

When you collect all components of a family, immediately **book** that family (place it face up in front of you) and score 1 point.

### END OF THE GAME

The game ends as soon as all families have been booked. The player who booked the most families wins. In case of a tie, all tied players share the victory.
